All About Us

Born from a love of fresh ingredients and community dining, Restro is more than just a place to eat—it's a place to connect.

Our Philosophy

It all started with a simple idea: create a welcoming space where families and friends can enjoy delicious, comforting food made from scratch. We believe in sourcing our ingredients locally whenever possible, supporting our community, and treating every guest like part of our family.

From our sizzling burgers to our Sunday roast, every dish is prepared with care and a passion for great flavor. We're not just serving meals; we're creating memories.
